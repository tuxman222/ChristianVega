If I Ever Become Homeless — Action Plan

Original question:

I'm homeless, I only have $10 to my name. How do I get back on my feet?

Step 1 – Protect the basics

Focus on shelter, food, and safety first.

  • Call 211 for homeless resources
  • Visit shelters or warming centers
  • Use libraries or police stations to ask for help

Food

  • Salvation Army
  • Catholic Charities
  • Soup kitchens
  • Sikh temples (free meals)
  • Community fridges

Save the $10 for emergency transportation.

Step 2 – Temporary income

Look for day labor:

  • PeopleReady
  • LaborWorks
  • HireQuest

Typical pay: $70–$120 per day.

Step 3 – Mailing address

  • Shelter address
  • Church outreach center
  • General Delivery at the post office

Step 4 – Replace documents

Ask outreach organizations for help replacing:

  • State ID
  • Birth certificate
  • Social Security card

Step 5 – Apply for assistance

  • SNAP (food stamps)
  • Medicaid
  • Temporary housing programs
  • Lifeline phone program

Step 6 – Build a small financial base

First goal: save $500–$1000.

Use shelters and food programs while working temporary jobs until you can rent a room.
