SOS

Personal contingency guide for emergency situations. Click a situation below to expand the recovery steps.

I have no money and no place to stay
  • Call 211 for local shelter resources
  • Visit libraries or police stations and ask for homeless outreach
  • Look for shelters, warming centers, or missions
  • Save any remaining cash for transportation
I need food immediately
  • Soup kitchens
  • Salvation Army
  • Catholic Charities
  • Sikh temples (free meals)
  • Community fridges
  • Food pantries
I need money fast
  • Day labor centers
  • PeopleReady
  • LaborWorks
  • HireQuest
  • Temporary employment agencies
I need a mailing address
  • Use a shelter address
  • Church outreach centers
  • General Delivery at the post office
I lost my ID or documents
  • Ask outreach organizations for help replacing:
  • State ID
  • Birth certificate
  • Social Security card
I need assistance programs
  • SNAP (food stamps)
  • Medicaid
  • Temporary housing programs
  • Lifeline phone program
Emergency and Help Hotline Numbers

Important U.S. help and crisis hotlines.

  • 911 – Emergency Services
    Police, fire, or medical emergency.
    Call 911
  • 988 – Suicide & Crisis Lifeline
    Mental health crisis support.
    Call or Text 988
  • 211 – Community Help Line
    Housing, shelters, food banks, social services.
    Call 211
  • 311 – City Services
    Local government services and non-emergency city help.
    Call 311
  • 988 + 1 – Veterans Crisis Line
    Veterans support and crisis counseling.
    Call 988 then press 1
  • 1-800-799-SAFE – National Domestic Violence Hotline
    1-800-799-7233
  • 1-800-656-HOPE – Sexual Assault Hotline (RAINN)
    1-800-656-4673
  • 1-800-RUNAWAY – National Runaway Safeline
    1-800-786-2929
  • 1-800-662-HELP – Substance Abuse & Mental Health Services
    1-800-662-4357
  • 1-800-222-1222 – Poison Control
    1-800-222-1222
  • 1-800-985-5990 – Disaster Distress Helpline
    1-800-985-5990
  • 1-888-373-7888 – Human Trafficking Hotline
    1-888-373-7888
  • 1-877-565-8860 – Trans Lifeline
    1-877-565-8860
  • 1-800-677-1116 – Elder Care Locator
    1-800-677-1116
